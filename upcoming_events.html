October 17

🎮 Game Night

We would like to organize a Game Night at the Student Playground to create a special evening for students, beyond the usual daily activities. The event will start at 9:00 PM, and we kindly request the venue to be closed one hour before the event to allow proper setup and ensure smooth organization. The goal is to provide a friendly environment where students can engage in competitions, socialize, and enjoy a unique atmosphere together, with background music enhancing the overall experience.

Participants
200 students
Open to
All Students
Time
9PM - 12AM
Location
Student Playground
Main Activities
  • FIFA Tournament - The tournament format will include brackets, and winners will advance until a final champion team is decided.
  • Chess Tournament - For students who prefer strategy and critical thinking. Matches will follow a knockout system.
  • Board Games - Including Monopoly, Uno, Rami Cards, and others, which will allow both casual and competitive play.
  • Billiard - Accessible for students who enjoy physical skill-based games.
Atmosphere & Hospitality
  • Cold drinks will be available for all participants, with ice and cups provided to keep the experience refreshing.
  • The event will focus on creating a welcoming and competitive yet friendly environment.
Requirements from SOLE
  • Board games.
  • Drinks, including ice and cups, to be provided for the event.
  • Event banner to promote the Game Night at the Student Playground.

Objectives of the Event
  • Build community spirit among students by combining both competitive and casual games.
  • Encourage teamwork throughthe group board games.
  • Offer an inclusive experience where everyone can find a game that suits their interest.
  • Make the Student Playground feel different and special for this night, compared to its usual daily use.

🏴‍☠️ Treasure Hunt

Interactive Treasure Hunt quiz competition powered by a web platform created by 1337 students. Participants will split into teams, each with their own profile on the platform. Teams will solve quizzes to earn XP, with a live leaderboard displaying the top performers. Victory can be achieved either by solving the big mystery quiz or by being the team with the highest XP. The top 3 teams will receive prizes.
